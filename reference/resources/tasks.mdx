A list of tasks.

<ParamField body="entries" type="Taskarray">
A list of tasks.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="total_count" type="integer(int64)">
example: `5000`

One greater than the offset of the last entry in the entire collection.
The total number of entries in the collection may be less than
`total_count`.
</ParamField>
