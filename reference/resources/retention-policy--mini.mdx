A mini representation of a retention policy, used when
nested within another resource.

* [Standard](/reference/resources/retention-policy/)
* [Mini](/reference/resources/retention-policy--mini/)
* [Base](/reference/resources/retention-policy--base/)

<Info>
This resource has a few variations that can be encountered when
using the API.

The fields that are part of the **mini** variant are generally
returned when the resource is nested within the response for
another resource.
</Info>

<ParamField body="id" type="string">
example: `12345`

The unique identifier that represents a retention policy.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="type" type="string">
example: `retention_policy`

The value will always be `retention_policy`.

Value is always `retention_policy`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="disposition_action" type="string">
example: `permanently_delete`

The disposition action of the retention policy.
This action can be `permanently_delete`, which
will cause the content retained by the policy
to be permanently deleted, or `remove_retention`,
which will lift the retention policy from the content,
allowing it to be deleted by users,
once the retention policy has expired.

Value is one of `permanently_delete`,`remove_retention`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="policy_name" type="string">
example: `Some Policy Name`

The name given to the retention policy.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="retention_length" type="string(int32)">
example: `365`

The length of the retention policy. This value
specifies the duration in days that the retention
policy will be active for after being assigned to
content. If the policy has a `policy_type` of
`indefinite`, the `retention_length` will also be
`indefinite`.
</ParamField>
